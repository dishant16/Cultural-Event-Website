Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT), known for its research and development, has emerged as the torch bearer among the premier technological institution of Gujarat over the past 20 years.

Spoural is a celebration - a celebration of culture, sports. It is about realising the underlying inherent junction of the three in an electrifying atmosphere. One of the most prominent college fests of Gujarat, it is a spectacle of our talent, aspirations, creativity and vision. Come 2020, we promise a bigger and better spoural.

Freedom of expresssion is given the most importance within these walls, and that is precisely what spoural stands for. spoural is a medium through which the youth can express thier views; be it through art, culture, sports skills, entertainment. It is a time when the student community comes forward to show its intensity, passion and enthusiasm.
